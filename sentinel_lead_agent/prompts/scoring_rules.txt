- Federal contracting, subcontracting, defense delivery, or agency support.

- Large enterprise scale.
- Mature internal compliance or security governance already advertised.
- No sign of regulated delivery, government work, or security/compliance exposure.
- Geography far outside the target focus without stronger offsetting signals.

Scoring bands:
- 1 to 3: weak fit.
- 4 to 6: moderate fit.
- 7 to 8: strong fit.
- 9 to 10: immediate priority.
