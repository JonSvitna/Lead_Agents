Increase score when evidence shows buyer-side compliance pressure:
- Federal contracting, subcontracting, defense delivery, or agency support as a contractor.
- CMMC, NIST 800-171, DFARS 252.204-7012, ITAR/EAR exposure, SAM.gov registration, CAGE code, CUI handling.
- Manufacturing, aerospace, engineering, R&D, or defense supply chain delivery.
- Small to mid-sized operating footprint with likely limited internal compliance capacity.

Decrease score when evidence shows:
- Large enterprise scale.
- Mature internal compliance or security governance already advertised.
- No sign of regulated delivery, government work, or buyer-side compliance exposure.
- Geography far outside the target focus without stronger offsetting signals.

Hard reject indicators (provider or competitor profile):
- C3PAO, RPO, CMMC consultant, CMMC auditing services, readiness provider, gap-assessment provider.
- Compliance-as-a-service, managed compliance, virtual CISO, vCISO, MSSP, MSP focused on compliance delivery.
- Marketing statements such as we help you achieve CMMC, our CMMC services, your CMMC partner, CMMC accelerator.

Scoring bands:
- 1 to 3: weak fit or provider/competitor.
- 4 to 6: moderate fit.
- 7 to 8: strong fit.
- 9 to 10: immediate priority buyer.
